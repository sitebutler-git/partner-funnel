# partner-funnel
Partner Funnels is an app created to allow creatives and marketers to connect with each other in a meaningful way and share resources (email lists). Once two partners (Partner A and Partner B) have made a connection and have shared names and email addresses, partner A will setup a "Partner Funnel" within their Funnel, as will partner B. Once the funnel link is setup, Parner A will forward the Funnel Link to Partner B, who will then send the link out to their email list (within a newsletter, featured in a blog post, video or podcast). Partner B's Audience will see the link via one of the above outlets. When the link is clicked within the media outlet, the audience member will be taken to the top of the funnel where they will be presented with a text or video "lead in" about Partner A. The have the opeion to leave their email address to progress to the next step in the funnel, receive more information (download a PDF, view a PDF, view a video, etc) at the bottom of that funnel step, the visitor is offerend a free "30 minute "get to know partener A" session along with a calendar to book the event.
